About Me

I am a driven and resourceful high school student with a strong passion for computer science and a desire to expand my skills through hands-on learning experiences. My journey in web development began at just 14 through SheCodes, a coding workshop for women, which provided me with my first glimpse into the vast world of computer science. This experience inspired me to deepen my knowledge and explore new opportunities.

In the 9th grade, I joined my school's Technology Student Association (TSA) and competed in the Webmaster event. When my team needed a User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) designer, I stepped up to the challenge. I taught myself UX/UI design and created a layout for our website, further strengthening my skills in web development. While my team did not win that competition, my commitment to learning and improving paid off in later TSA competitions, where my designs and Figma prototypes contributed to our team placing in the top 10.

In July 2024, I was accepted into Harvard's Pre-College program, where I spent two weeks studying Digital Media. This experience highlighted for me how essential computer science is in modern life, from creating visual content to designing collaborative workspaces with coding and data analysis. The program confirmed my aspirations to become a Front-End Web Developer and pursue a future that combines my technical skills with my creative vision.
I aim to study UX/UI design to develop interactive and user-centered websites and software that enhance the online experience.

UX/UI Design Projects

Fresh Start

2023

I created a mock app, Fresh Start, using Figma. Fresh Start was semi-functional in that button, scrolling, and text-input fucntions could be used. I also conducted user research among a class of graduating high school seniors to undrstand their preferences and needs.

Renewable Revolution

2023

I created the layout for the Renewable Revolution, a website promoting environmental alternatives and solutions, including the color palettes, typography, and iconography. I also designed the website's functionality through prototyping and assisted web developers in building the site.

Herbivore Haven

2025

I created the layout for the Herbivore Haven, a website promoting a vegetarian restaurant, for the 2025 TSA (Technology Student Association) Webmaster challenge. This included designing the color palettes, typography, and iconography. I also helped curate the menu and image choices used for the website.

SymbioAI

2025

I created the layout for SymbioAI, a chatbot meant to help users be more environmentally conscious. SymbioAI was built for the 2025 TSA Software Development competion. In addition to being the lead UX/UI designer, I also designed the brand identity, such as the logo, color palette, font choice, and smaller icons.
